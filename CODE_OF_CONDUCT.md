# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or public shaming.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions
that are not aligned with this policy.

## Reporting

To report violations, open a private security issue if available or contact the maintainers.
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled fairly.
